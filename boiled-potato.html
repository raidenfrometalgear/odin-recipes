Boiled Potato

Description

Hi guys, welcome to my website today I will be teaching you how to make boiled potatoes

If you guys have any problem with following the recipe, please contact me through my email : shakyaa130b@gmail.com

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Water

Steps

Before you start get these tools ready
  • Potato peeler
  • Chopping board
  • Knife
  • Pot
Now that your tools are ready follow the instructions below
  • Peel the potatoes
  • Chop the potatoes (by cutting the potatoes to smaller pieces you can cook them quicker!)
  • Boil Water on the pot
  • Put Potatoes on the pot
  • Cook the potatoes for a minimum of 10 minutes, you can keep it for longer if you want your potatoes soft!
    • OPTIONAL STEPS
    You can add salt on the water or the potatoes if you like
